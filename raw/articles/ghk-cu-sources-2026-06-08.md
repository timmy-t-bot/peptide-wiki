## Safety Information

- GHK-Cu has been used in cosmetics for decades without reported adverse effects
- Copper (II) redox activity is silenced when complexed with GHK — non-toxic copper delivery
- Wilson disease is an absolute contraindication (copper accumulation disorder)
- No long-term systemic human safety data exists for injectable use
- The peptide stimulates both MMPs and TIMPs (dual regulation of tissue remodeling)
- Anti-cancer gene expression profiles observed (caspase activation, growth regulatory genes)
- Animal studies: no significant systemic toxicity reported (Bian et al. 2024 silicosis study explicitly noted "without significant systemic toxicity")
- Mendias & Awan (2026) review: discusses safety of unapproved peptide therapies including GHK-Cu
