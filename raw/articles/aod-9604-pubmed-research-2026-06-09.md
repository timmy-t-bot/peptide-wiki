## Key Papers

### 1. Heffernan M et al. Endocrinology 2001;142(12):5182-9. PMID: 11713213
**The effects of human GH and its lipolytic fragment (AOD9604) on lipid metabolism following chronic treatment in obese mice and beta(3)-AR knock-out mice.**

Key findings:
- Both hGH and AOD9604 capable of inducing weight loss and increasing lipolytic sensitivity following long-term treatment in mice
- Mechanism via interaction with beta-adrenergic pathway, particularly beta-3-adrenergic receptors (β3-AR)
- 14 days chronic ip administration reduced body weight and body fat in obese mice
- Correlated with increased β3-AR RNA expression
- hGH and AOD9604 increased repressed β3-AR RNA in obese mice to levels comparable with lean mice
- In β3-AR knockout mice: long-term treatment with hGH and AOD9604 FAILED to produce change in body weight and increase lipolysis observed in wild-type
- However: acute AOD9604 increased energy expenditure and fat oxidation even in β3-AR knock-out mice
- CONCLUSION: Lipolytic actions NOT mediated directly through β3-AR, but both compounds increase β3-AR expression contributing to enhanced lipolytic sensitivity

### 2. Cox HD et al. Drug Test Anal 2015;7(1):31-8. PMID: 25208511
**Detection and in vitro metabolism of AOD9604.**

Key findings:
- AOD9604 = C-terminal fragment hGH 177-191 + additional tyrosine at N-terminus
- Mimics lipolytic properties of GH without diabetogenic side effects
- Banned by WADA
- Available on internet websites; identified in confiscated vials in USA
- Solid-phase extraction method validated: LOD 50 pg/mL in urine
- Good linearity, precision (<20%), specificity, recovery (62%)
- Six potential metabolites identified from incubation in serum and urine
- Single metabolite CRSVEGSCG significantly more stable than parent
- Screening for AOD9604 + stable metabolite may allow increased detection window

### 3. Orlovius AK et al. Drug Test Anal 2013;5(11-12):850-2. PMID: 24124033
**AOD-9604 does not influence the WADA hGH isoform immunoassay.**
- Important for anti-doping: AOD-9604 use does not interfere with hGH isoform test

### 4. Thomas A et al. J Sep Sci 2016;39(2):333-41. PMID: 26578461
**Simplifying and expanding the screening for peptides <2 kDa by direct urine injection, LC, and ion mobility MS.**
- AOD-9604 listed among prohibited substances covered by WADA
- Listed alongside GHRPs, Desmopressin, LH-RH, Buserelin, Triptorelin, TB-500, Anamorelin, etc.
- LOD 50-500 pg/mL; covers MRPL of 2 ng/mL
- Method precise (imprecision <20%), linear 0-10 ng/mL
- -20°C appropriate storage temperature for urine samples

### 8. Kwon DR, Park GY. Ann Clin Lab Sci 2015;45(4):426-32. PMID: 26275694
**Effect of Intra-articular Injection of AOD9604 with or without Hyaluronic Acid in Rabbit Osteoarthritis Model.**
- Rabbit OA model: AOD9604 intra-articular injections enhanced cartilage regeneration
- Combined AOD9604 + HA more effective than either alone
- Reduced lameness period significantly

### 10. Wilding J. Curr Opin Investig Drugs 2004;5(4):436-40. PMID: 15134286

### 11. Valentino MA et al. Clin Pharmacol Ther 2010;87(6):652-662. PMID: 20445536
