GHRP-2 (Growth Hormone Releasing Peptide-2) is a GHS-R1a receptor agonist.
Mechanism: Stimulates GH release by acting on the ghrelin receptor and synergistically with GHRH.
Evidence: Potent GH elevation in humans. Animal models show tendon healing and muscle hypertrophy.
Comparison: Less appetite-stimulating than GHRP-6, more potent for GH secretion.
Safety: Potential for prolactin elevation and cortisol increase.
