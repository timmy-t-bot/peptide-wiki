FOXO4-DRI is a D-retro-inverso peptide designed to disrupt the interaction between FOXO4 and p53. 
In senescent cells, FOXO4 normally sequesters p53, preventing it from inducing apoptosis. 
FOXO4-DRI displaces p53, allowing the senescent cell to undergo apoptosis (senolysis).
Research in mice showed that clearing p16-positive senescent cells led to improved healthspan and reduced age-related pathology.
However, a critical safety signal emerged: in certain models, the systemic clearance of senescent cells was associated with an increased risk of pulmonary hypertension or vascular issues, likely due to the role of senescent cells in maintaining some vascular niches.
